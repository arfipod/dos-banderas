# Dos Banderas Game Rules

## Concept

The player is a person walking through concrete life situations. Each round is a small decision. The central tension is whether the player acts under Christ's Banner or accepts a shortcut offered by the Dark Banner.

The game is not about solving all of life in one dramatic moment. It is about repeated discernment in small trials.

## Objective

Complete five Life Trials and then overcome the Final Choice.

You win if:

- Communion is 5 or higher.
- Attachment is below 7.
- The Final Choice is successfully overcome.

If Communion reaches 7 or higher, the result is a full victory: **One in God**.

## Meters

| Meter | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Communion | Union with God and others. |
| Attachment | Disordered dependency, false security, or interior chains. |
| Desolation | Confusion, weariness, discouragement, and spiritual heaviness. |
| Consolation | Clarity, hope, ordered desire, and encouragement. |
| Fruits | Practical growth used to buy better cards. |

## Round Structure

1. Reveal one Life Trial.
2. Reveal one Dark Banner card.
3. Choose Christ's Banner or accept the shortcut.
4. Play up to 4 cards.
5. Apply conditional effects manually if needed.
6. Resolve Light × Fervor against Darkness.
7. Buy from the Church Row.

## Resolution Formula

```text
Result = Light × Fervor
```

Fervor starts at 1 each round.

## Outcomes

### Clean Victory

Condition: success without accepting a shortcut.

Reward:

- +1 Communion
- +3 Fruits

### Mixed Victory

Condition: success after accepting a shortcut.

Reward:

- +2 Fruits
- +1 Attachment

### Failure with Examen

Condition: failure, but Examen or Reconciliation was played.

Reward/consequence:

- +1 Desolation
- +1 Consolation
- +1 Fruit

### Closed Failure

Condition: failure without Examen.

Consequence:

- +1 Desolation

## Buying Cards

At the end of the round, the player may buy from the Church Row.

| Card Type | Cost |
|---|---:|
| Weapon of Light | 1 |
| Virtue | 2 |
| Gift of the Holy Spirit | 3 |
| Sacrament | 4 |
| Ally | 4 |
| Mystery | 5 |

## Manual Effects

The simulator intentionally does not fully automate every theological or conditional effect. It provides manual modifiers for:

- +1 / -1 Light
- +1 / -1 Fervor
- +1 / -1 Darkness
- meter adjustments

This is intentional for early playtesting. The text of each card remains the source of truth.
